Noriko Tajima

Noriko Tajima has blue, straight, short hair with white eyes. She wear the school's defualt uniform with blue stockings.

 

Personality

Noriko Tajima is a student of class 1-1. Noriko sometimes spends time talking with Eriko Sakura and Meisa Ito. After school, she spends time on the baseball field with Keishi Onozawa.

When talking to Akari, Noriko says that she's going to meet her senpai (Keishi) in the baseball field. She recently joined the sports club, probably only to stay with Keishi. But he seems not to love her back (or at least, not yet).

When she sees Akari killing someone, she runs away.
