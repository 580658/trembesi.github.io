Tomio Nakano

Tomio Nakano is a male student of Yandere School.

He's a part of the computer club, so he's often talking with the head. For example: before lessons, they go talking on the rooftop, and they talk about programming computers. He says he'd like to create a game about zombies, This May Be A Hint To Tea And Cake's Next Game.

He Is The Only Gay character In The Game.

 

Appearance

He's tall and he's got straight green hair and green eyes. He's got a fair complexion.

 

NOTE

He can be pushed off the roof, along with the club head and Angelina (chapter 2)
